I wish sleep didn't have to feel like a luxury. Sleep, at its core, is unadulterated, unfiltered, me-time. When I sleep, I make no compromises. When I sleep, my body focuses on healing myself. So why is it that everything in my life seems to demand the extinguishment of my sleep?
